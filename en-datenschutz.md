# Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains the nature, scope, and purpose of processing personal data on this website in accordance with Articles 13 and 14 GDPR.

## 1. Controller

The controller responsible for data processing on this website is:

**Ilka Warstat-Schibilak**  
E-mail: [info@kinderklassik.com](mailto:info@kinderklassik.com)

## 2. Hosting and provision of the website

This website is provided via **GitHub Pages**. When accessing the website, the hosting provider automatically processes information in so-called server log files. This may include in particular:

- IP address (possibly shortened/temporary)
- date and time of access
- requested page/file
- referrer URL
- browser type and browser version
- operating system

Processing is based on Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in secure, stable, and error-free provision of the website).

## 3. Content and third-party services

### 3.1 External fonts and icon libraries

To ensure a consistent display, this website uses external resources:

- **Google Fonts** (provided by Google Ireland Limited)
- **Font Awesome via cdnjs.cloudflare.com** (provided by Cloudflare, Inc.)

When these resources are loaded, your IP address may be transmitted to the respective providers because your browser retrieves the files directly from their servers.

The legal basis is Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in a technically consistent and appealing presentation).

### 3.2 Embedded content

If embedded content is used on individual pages (e.g., maps, videos, or external media), the data protection provisions of the respective third-party providers also apply. Retrieving such content may involve the transfer of personal data (in particular IP address and technical metadata).

## 4. Cookies and similar technologies

This website does not use tracking or marketing cookies.

However, technically necessary local browser storage may be used (e.g., to store display settings such as light/dark mode). This storage serves only user-friendly presentation and is based on Article 6(1)(f) GDPR.

## 5. Contact

If you contact us by e-mail, the data you provide (e.g., e-mail address, name, message content) will be processed to handle your request.

Legal bases:

- Article 6(1)(b) GDPR (pre-contractual measures / performance of a contract), if your request is contract-related, or
- Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in handling other inquiries).

Data will be stored only as long as necessary to process your request or as required by statutory retention obligations.

## 6. Legal bases for processing

Unless otherwise stated in this privacy policy, personal data is processed on the basis of the following legal grounds:

- Article 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent)
- Article 6(1)(b) GDPR (contract / pre-contractual measures)
- Article 6(1)(c) GDPR (legal obligation)
- Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest)

## 7. Recipients and third-country transfers

Personal data is transferred to third parties only insofar as this is technically necessary for operating the website or legally required.

When using services based outside the EU/EEA, transfer to third countries (especially the USA) cannot be ruled out. In such cases, transfer takes place on the basis of appropriate safeguards pursuant to Articles 44 et seq. GDPR, where applicable.

## 8. Storage period

Personal data is deleted as soon as the purpose of storage no longer applies and no statutory retention obligations prevent deletion.

## 9. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you are entitled in particular to the following rights:

- access to your stored data (Article 15 GDPR)
- rectification of inaccurate data (Article 16 GDPR)
- erasure of your data (Article 17 GDPR)
- restriction of processing (Article 18 GDPR)
- data portability (Article 20 GDPR)
- objection to processing (Article 21 GDPR)
- withdrawal of consent with effect for the future (Article 7(3) GDPR)

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority (Article 77 GDPR).

## 10. Data security

Technical and organizational measures are used to protect personal data against loss, manipulation, unauthorized access, and other risks.

## 11. Changes to this privacy policy

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect legal or technical changes. The currently published version on this website applies.
